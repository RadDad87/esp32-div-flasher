How to Flash

  1. Connect your ESP32-DIV via USB
  2. Select your board version above (v1 or v2)
  3. Click Flash on the firmware you want
  4. Select the COM/serial port in the popup
  5. Wait for the flash to complete (~60 seconds)
If the device isn't detected, hold the BOOT button while plugging in USB, then try again.
